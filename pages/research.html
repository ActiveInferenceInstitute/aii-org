Our Work

The Active Inference Institute conducts and supports open-science research on the theoretical advancement, cognitive modelling, and practical applications of the Free Energy Principle and Active Inference.

Future Directions

As we look to 2026 and beyond, the Institute is expanding foundational work bridging theory with real-world deployment — from cognitive modelling and AI safety to robotics and complex networks.

  • 6th Applied Active Inference Symposium — Planning underway. symposium.activeinference.institute
  • IWAI 2026 — The 7th International Workshop on Active Inference, focusing on "Foundations" across computational theory, cognitive modelling, and empirical applications.
  • Industry Partnerships — Actively seeking collaborators to translate foundational science into operational planning and decision-making platforms.
