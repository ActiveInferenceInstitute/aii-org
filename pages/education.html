Textbook Groups

The Institute organises recurring, collaborative reading groups around the two core textbooks. Over 7 cohorts have completed the cycle. Register for the next cohort →

Active Inference: The Free Energy Principle in Mind, Brain, and Behavior

Thomas Parr, Giovanni Pezzulo & Karl Friston · MIT Press, 2022

The foundational textbook presenting the mathematical and conceptual framework for Active Inference. Participants engage in peer-to-peer discussion, unpack complex mathematical formulae, and contextualise biological case studies.

MIT Press →

Fundamentals of Active Inference: Principles, Algorithms, and Applications of the Free Energy Principle for Engineers

Sanjeev V. Namjoshi · MIT Press, 2025 · ISBN 9780262050951

An engineering-focused companion developed in collaboration with the Institute since 2023, endorsed by Karl Friston. The 12 interactive simulations in this website accompany this textbook, providing hands-on exploration of core concepts.
